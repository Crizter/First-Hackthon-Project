Transportation

The SRM University provides safe air-conditioned/ non-air-conditioned transport for students, with a communication system and supervised pick
up and dispersal. Though, the Campus is located in a well-connected place. There is the easy availability of public conveyance for students who
come from Delhi/ Sonipat and other parts of Haryana. The campus is just a few kilometers away from Delhi.
